Selected Projects & Achievements

Amazon Profit Analysis Project

In my Business Analytics class, I worked on a project that analyzed Amazon’s online profitability. My team used real data to study how costs, prices, and customer growth affected profits. We built charts and models to see which factors had the biggest impact on performance and suggested ways Amazon could improve efficiency and increase revenue. This project helped me learn how to use data to solve real business problems and make clear, practical recommendations.

View Project

RentalVestor Website

RentalVestor is a website I created to help real estate investors make smarter decisions. It lets users analyze rental properties by entering key information like purchase price, rent, taxes, and expenses. The site then calculates important metrics such as cash flow, return on investment, and cap rate. I built it to combine my interest in business analytics and real estate, using data to show investors how different factors affect profitability. RentalVestor makes it easier for people to understand the financial performance of rental properties before they invest.

Visit Website

Rental Property Endeavor

I partnered with my father to invest in a rental property where I helped manage the renovation, budgeting, and tenant placement process. I analyzed local market data, including home prices, rent levels, and vacancy rates, to choose a strong investment location. During the project, I learned how to balance expenses with expected returns and how to make data-driven decisions about pricing and improvements. This experience gave me a hands-on understanding of real estate investing, property management, and how analytics can be used to increase long-term value.
